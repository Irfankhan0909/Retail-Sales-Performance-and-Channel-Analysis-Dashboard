Retail Sales Performance & Channel Analysis (E-commerce Stores)

**Objective**  
Analyze retail sales data to understand **revenue trends, customer behavior, and sales channels** for better business decision-making.

**Tools Used**  
Excel, Power pivot, pivot tables, formulas, analytics

**Key Work**
- Cleaned and prepared raw sales and customer data  
- Created KPIs for **sales, orders, and customer segments**  
- Built dashboards to track **monthly trends, top products, and channel performance**

**Business Value**  
Helps management identify **high-performing channels, best-selling products, and customer demand patterns**.
